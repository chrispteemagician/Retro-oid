What Can I Identify?

🎮 Gaming

Consoles, cartridges, handhelds, arcade PCBs

💾 Computers

ZX Spectrum, BBC Micro, C64, Amiga, Atari ST

🤖 Toys

Star Wars, He-Man, Transformers G1, TMNT

📚 Media

Retro magazines, comics, cassettes, floppy disks

📸 Best Photo Tips

✓ Good natural or artificial light — no flash glare
✓ Include any labels, serial numbers or cartridge labels
✓ Show the full item — not just a corner
✓ Multiple items? Identify them one at a time for best results
